PaliFest

A Celebration of Palestine

Three Day Event

When: May 16, May 17 & May 18

Where: Toyota Pavillion at Concord in Concord, CA

KNOW BEFORE YOU GO

The festival is a 3 day festival. Traffic is to be expected during those 3 days. Plan your activies accordingly. If parking spots are full, utilize the parking space by Safeway in the Clayton Valley Shopping Center. Rideshare is reccomended. Buses are also available to use for free. BART does not go to Toyota Pavillion at Concord

Event is expected to begin at 2:00PM PST. Please arrive as early as you can. You MUST show your ticket at the entrance or else no entry. If you have any questions, contact the box office at the Toyota Pavillion at Concord. You can also call this number: (925) 676-8742 and a team member should be with you.
